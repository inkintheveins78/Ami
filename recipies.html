Breakfast recipe: Scrambled Eggs


Ingredients:

  • 4-5 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper, any amount

    • Gets Roughly 2 servings
  1. Beat the eggs, milk, salt and pepper in a small or medium bowl, until it looks mixed.
  2. Pour the eggs into a pan, any size you want.
  3. As the eggs start to look like scrambled eggs, pull the eggs across the pan.
  4. Continue cooking them until there is no liquid left. Remove from the heat.
  5. Serve them immediately.
